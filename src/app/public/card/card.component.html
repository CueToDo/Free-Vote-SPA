Make whipping illegal

When you vote in an election, it’s illegal for anyone to bribe, bully or blackmail you to change your vote. You have a free vote in a secret ballot to protect you against anyone seeking to force you to vote differently.

However, the same protection does not apply to your representative when they get to parliament. MPs very rarely get a free vote and there are no secret ballots. Party whips are there to enforce "party discipline". Party interests come first. That means power sits with a small group of people who set the agenda and have no interest in listening to the people's representatives.

Bribing, bullying and blackmailing (whipping) is undemocratic and should be a criminal offence: Perverting the course of democracy.

Representatives must have a free vote at all times
